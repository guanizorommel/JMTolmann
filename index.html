About Us

JM Tolmann Laboratories was established in 1994 and become one of the national pharmaceutical engaged in the Manufacture, Marketing and Distribution of Quality Branded medicines. Through the unwavering support from the Marketing Division the very first batch of medicines has rolled out from the Manufacturing Division ready to be delivered to our major clients.

At the middle of the first quarter of 2006, the Board of Directors has made a decisive move for the Marketing and manufacturing Division to operate independently to be able for the two divisions to focused more on its individual programs.Thus, eventually continue the efficiency, competitiveness and further enhance marketing prospects of Marketing Division.

On the other hand, the Manufacturing Division is now focused on Toll Manufacturing and Developing new products as part of its Product Development Program recently, we enthusiastic launch a drive with a motive of capturing a greater opportunity to have alarge clientele network with an objective of developing new partners in this field, consequently, maintaining a tradition of producing quality and affordable medicines geared towards customer satisfaction.

Vision

To become a competitive Manufacturer thru the implementation of Quality Management System (QMS) by producing quality, safe, effective and affordable medicine to be distributed globally to serve the health of mankind.

Mission

Implement cGMP and produce only the safest and most efficacious and affordable products.

Constantly support and develop our people by enhancing quality and technical competence through comprehensive training.
